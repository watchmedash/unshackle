A Slice of Friendship

When Pizza Turned into a Life Lesson

Posted by aritocraticattitude on November 26, 2024

Back in 2014 or 2015, when Papa John’s first opened in the UAE, they launched an exciting *Buy 1 Get 1 Free* offer. Little did I know, this promotion would lead to one of the most unforgettable and frustrating nights of my life!

The Invitation

It was a long day. I had just returned home after a grueling 1.5-hour drive from work in Dubai. As the sole breadwinner, every dirham counted, and I preferred the comfort of home-cooked meals by my mom. That evening, a friend called, insisting we visit Papa John’s in Ajman. Despite my reluctance, he convinced me, promising we'd enjoy dinner together.

The Wait and the Shock

After picking him up and reaching the outlet, we discovered a two-hour wait. Hungry and exhausted, I stayed patient. When the pizzas finally arrived, my friend casually mentioned, *“By the way, the second pizza is for my girlfriend, and this one’s for me and my brothers.”*

I was stunned. Here I was, running on empty after sacrificing my evening, and all I got was one single slice of pizza offered half-heartedly. Frustrated, I refused the slice, dropped him off, and didn’t speak to him for three years.

“Friendship is about give and take, not just take and take!”

The Aftermath

Years later, my friend apologized sincerely, and we rebuilt our bond. Today, he’s one of my closest friends, even though he now lives in London. Ironically, his family owns five famous cafeterias in Sharjah, voted the best shawarma spots. Go figure!

Looking back, it’s a story we both laugh about—a reminder of how friendships evolve and sometimes grow stronger after moments of misunderstanding. And yes, I still tease him about the pizza incident!
